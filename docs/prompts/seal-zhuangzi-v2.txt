A single traditional Chinese carved stone seal imprint, photographed in pure isolation, centered on a completely clean pure white background.

The seal contains EXACTLY ONE Chinese character, and that character MUST be 莊 (traditional form of 庄, pinyin: zhuāng, meaning "solemn, Zhuang surname"). This is a classical Han / Qin dynasty small seal script (秦汉小篆) rendering of the TRADITIONAL form 莊.

STRUCTURE of the character 莊 in small seal script — two stacked zones top-to-bottom:
  • TOP zone: 艸 / 艹 grass radical — in small seal script this appears as TWO small curving plant-like strokes side by side, each looking like a short vertical stem with a tiny leaf curling off. Together they form a compact "grass" header.
  • BOTTOM zone: 壯 (zhuàng, "strong") — composed of LEFT component 爿 (a narrow vertical pictograph like a bed on its side, with three horizontal notches) and RIGHT component 士 (scholar, rendered as one long horizontal on top, a vertical pillar, and one long horizontal at the bottom). The 爿 and 士 are placed side by side horizontally within the bottom zone.
Total: small grass radical on top, then 壯 (a horizontal two-part compound) beneath. Three vertically-arranged "layers" when the 壯 is read.

CRITICAL — what the character is NOT:
  • NOT simply 壯 without the grass radical on top
  • NOT 士 doubled, NOT 世, NOT 壯士 (two separate characters side by side)
  • NOT any compound that reads horizontally as two separate characters
  • NOT any other character — only 莊

The seal is pressed in vermilion cinnabar red paste (朱砂印泥), raised positive relief (阳文 yang wen) carving. Simple square red border frame with imperfect hand-stamped edges. Natural uneven ink distribution, slight bleeding, imperfect spots.

Square format, seal occupies the center 70% of the frame. Slightly rotated -2 degrees. Pure clean white seamless background.

Photography style: museum artifact documentation from a classical Chinese seal catalog (印谱), even soft diffused studio lighting.

Color palette: pure white background, vermilion cinnabar red (approximately #C8342B) only.
