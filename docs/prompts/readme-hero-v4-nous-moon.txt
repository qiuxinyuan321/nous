A cover-grade ultra-minimal INTP painting on aged rice paper (xuanzhi), warm off-white #f6f1e6 with natural fiber grain and very faint random water stains, 2560×1440 wide banner.

OVERALL INTENT: a quiet philosophical landscape without any landscape — a single contemplative mind-field. Strong top-to-bottom narrative: thought originates above, descends through stillness, lands below. 65% negative space. Every element has reason; nothing is decoration.

=== UPPER THIRD (origin of thought) ===
- ONE pale ink-wash circular moon halo, positioned slightly upper-left (about 30% from left, 22% from top), diameter roughly 1/5 of canvas width. NOT a realistic moon — a soft grey ink fog (15-25% opacity charcoal), edges undefined, bleeding gently into paper. No craters, no shading, no glowing rim.
- AT THE MOON'S HEART: ONE small solid cinnabar-red (#c8432e) square seal stamp, clean edges, no text inside, pure color block. Size ≈ 3% of canvas width. This is THE visual anchor of the entire image.
- AROUND the moon and extending to its right across the upper half: a sparse abstract node-network — 20-25 small geometric glyphs (solid dots, hollow circles, thin-outline squares, hexagons, triangles, tiny cross marks, diamond dots). Nodes are clearly grouped into loose constellations, not uniformly scattered. 
- 3-4 key nodes in the upper half are larger cinnabar-red square stamps (but noticeably smaller than the moon's heart stamp — the heart stamp must remain the most prominent red).
- Connecting lines: extremely thin hairlines (gray #8a8a7a at 30-40% opacity), straight or very gently curved, irregular topology.

=== MIDDLE BAND (the horizon of stillness, around 56-60% from top) ===
- ONE single horizontal ink line spanning ~55% of canvas width, centered. Extremely thin (0.5-1px equivalent), at 22-28% opacity charcoal. Slightly uneven stroke quality — hand-drawn feel, not mechanical. NOT a border. NOT a frame edge. Just a whisper of a ground.
- Above this line: the node network fades into emptier paper near the line.
- Below this line: huge empty breathing space.

=== LOWER THIRD (where thought lands) ===
- Much emptier than upper. This is the "rest" region.
- 2-3 small cinnabar-red square stamps scattered widely (not clustered) below the horizon, like lanterns that have settled on ground. Same color as upper reds.
- Very few tiny node glyphs (maximum 5-6 pieces) — a whisper of arrival, not a second network.
- A single extremely faint ink droplet stain somewhere in lower-right, about 15% opacity grey, no detail.
- Preserve vast empty rice-paper below.

=== LEFT-UPPER CORNER (DNA anchor) ===
- One soft organic ink-wash cloud at 20-35% opacity charcoal, no brush-stroke detail, just atmospheric fog that bleeds into paper. Shape asymmetric and natural. Same treatment as the prior hero version — this is the visual DNA link.

=== PALETTE (strictly limited) ===
- Dominant: warm paper off-white (#f6f1e6)
- Accent reds: cinnabar (#c8432e) — moon's heart stamp + 3-4 upper red seals + 2-3 lower red seals
- Ink shapes: deep charcoal (#1a1a1a) for node glyphs
- Hairlines + fog: cool soft grey (#8a8a7a)
- NO other colors anywhere. NO gold, NO blue, NO green.

=== STRICTLY FORBIDDEN ===
- NO text of any kind — NO Chinese characters, NO English letters, NO Greek letters (no νοῦς glyphs rendered), NO numbers, NO captions, NO signature
- NO people, NO faces, NO silhouettes, NO human figures, NO body parts
- NO mountains, NO landscape scenery, NO shanshui 山水, NO trees, NO buildings
- NO brushes, NO ink stones, NO paper scrolls as subject
- NO calligraphy strokes as primary element (fog and horizon are the only ink-wash elements)
- NO decorative borders, frames, corner ornaments, flower patterns, dragons, phoenixes, clouds-as-shape, temples
- NO realistic moon details (keep moon as abstract halo only)
- NO dense crowding — preserve massive negative space, especially in lower third
- NO ancient Chinese scenery elements
- NO watercolor gradient backgrounds — base stays clean paper texture

=== MOOD ===
"Nous" — the Greek word for pure intellect, the mind's highest faculty. This image is that word made visible: the moon as the source of intellect (with a red seal at its heart, like a stamped identity), a constellation of thoughts orbiting it, a quiet horizon line, and a few thoughts that have found their place on the ground below. Not busy. Not decorative. A thinker's stillness.

STYLE REFERENCE: minimalist data-visualization + Eastern ink-wash restraint + Bauhaus geometric clarity + contemplative Zen page layout. Think of a single page from a philosopher's notebook, left open at dusk.
