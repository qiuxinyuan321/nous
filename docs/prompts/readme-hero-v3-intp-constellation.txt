Full-width abstract knowledge constellation diagram, ultra-minimal INTP analytical aesthetic.

CANVAS: 2560×1440 wide banner. Texture: aged rice paper (xuanzhi), warm off-white #f6f1e6 base, subtle natural fiber grain, very faint random water stains, NO ornamental patterns.

CONTENT: a sparse abstract node-network / star-chart. About 30-40 geometric node glyphs scattered across the whole canvas with careful negative space — not clustered, not centered. Node shapes mix: small solid circles, thin-outlined squares, hexagons, hollow triangles, tiny cross marks, diamond dots. Asymmetric organic distribution, like a constellation or knowledge graph, with clear empty regions.

CONNECTIONS: extremely thin hairline ink strokes (gray #8a8a7a at 40% opacity) linking nodes in an irregular topology — some nodes highly connected, many with only 1-2 edges, occasional isolated nodes. NO curved decorative flourishes. Straight or very gentle-arc lines only.

ACCENT: 5-7 key nodes are solid cinnabar-red (#c8432e, a traditional Chinese seal color) square stamps, slightly larger than other nodes, no text inside — pure color blocks as visual anchors.

ATMOSPHERE: one single faint ink-wash blur in a corner (lower-left or upper-right), grey mist at 15% opacity, no detailed brushstrokes, just soft fog. Dominant color palette: paper warm-white, deep charcoal ink (#1a1a1a), hairline cool-grey, cinnabar red.

MOOD: calm, analytical, intellectual, minimalist, breathing space. Looks like a thinker's notebook page or an astronomical chart, NOT like a painting.

STRICT EXCLUSIONS (do NOT render ANY of these):
- NO mountains, landscapes, shanshui 山水
- NO brushes, ink stones, scrolls, calligraphy strokes
- NO people, faces, figures, silhouettes
- NO text, Chinese characters, English letters, numbers, captions
- NO decorative borders, frames, flower patterns, dragons, temples, clouds as shapes
- NO ancient Chinese scene elements whatsoever
- NO watercolor gradients as subject; keep ink-wash to minimal background haze only
- NO busy detail; preserve huge negative space

STYLE REFERENCE: minimalist data visualization + Eastern ink restraint + Bauhaus geometric clarity. Think: a page from an INTP's abstract thinking notebook.
